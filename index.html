Roman Time 2025

January 16-20, 2025 | San Jose, CA

About the Event

The Roman Time convention offers a captivating exploration of the culture, history, and innovations of ancient Rome.
Attendees will delve into the grandeur of Roman civilization, learning about its influence on modern society through engaging sessions, hands-on activities, and discussions led by experts in the field.

Event Highlights

  • Interactive reenactments of Roman daily life, including gladiatorial games and public forums.
  • Workshops on Roman architecture, engineering, and the legacy of the aqueducts.
  • Expert-led talks on Roman law, philosophy, and its lasting impact on Western governance.
  • Opportunities to experience Roman culinary traditions with historical recipes and tastings.
Event details, including the schedule and location, will be shared during registration to ensure an exclusive experience and to prioritize those with a genuine interest in the convention.
